One of the hardest things about this project was definitely figuring out the dice. I had to individually plot out each point on the dice and draw that in a show method. However each one of those dots on the dice had to be on a variable so I could put the dice anywhere on the screen. Another thing that I struggled on is the nested for loop putting my dice on the screen. The reason it was so hard is because I had never really done nested for loops before. To figure this one out I just wrote out what I thought was happening on a sheet of paper and brained my way through it.
